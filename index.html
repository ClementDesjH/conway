Conway's game of life is a zero-player game based on the following 5 rules:

  1. A Black Square is considered alive
  2. A White Square is considered dead
  3. If a Black Square has 2 or 3 live neighbors, it lives on to the next iteration
  4. Otherwise it dies, due to under/overpopulation
  5. A dead cell comes to life if it has exactly 3 alive neighbors
